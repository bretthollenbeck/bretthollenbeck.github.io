Hello! I am an Associate Professor at UCLA Anderson School of Management. My research is in empirical industrial organization and quantitative marketing.

My recent work studies the market for fake reviews on Amazon, the financial consequences of legalized sports gambling, and the impact of AI on consumer search.

My recent working papers have been covered in The Economist, New York Times, The Atlantic, and Last Week Tonight.
